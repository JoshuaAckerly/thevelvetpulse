# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Graveyard Jokes Studios ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit The Velvet Pulse (thevelvetpulse.graveyardjokes.com).

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit this site, we automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate geographic location
- Browser type and version
- Visit timestamps and page views
- Referrer information

When you use the contact form, we collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Understand site usage and improve user experience
- Monitor site performance and identify issues
- Respond to contact form inquiries
- Ensure site security

## 3. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

- **AWS S3** — for image and asset storage
- **Zoho Mail** — for sending email responses to contact inquiries

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

## 4. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for basic site functionality. No tracking cookies are set beyond what is required for the site to operate. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.

## 5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Visit data is used for aggregate analytics and is not retained indefinitely.

## 6. Your Rights

If you have questions about data we may have collected or wish to request deletion, contact us at [admin@graveyardjokes.com](mailto:admin@graveyardjokes.com).

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
